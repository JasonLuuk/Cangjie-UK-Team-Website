About Cangjie UK Team

We are a team based in the Edinburgh focused on the Cangjie programming language — sharing tutorials, writing blogs, and building practical resources for developers.

Who we are

Cangjie UK Team brings together developers and researchers interested in high-performance, type-safe systems programming with Cangjie. We create learning materials, open-source projects, and community content to help others get started and go deeper.

What we do

We maintain this site with Blogs (project write-ups and technical posts), News (releases and updates), and links to the official Cangjie tutorial and channels. You can follow our work here and through the official Cangjie community.

Get in touch

For the language itself, visit the official Cangjie website. For tutorials and docs, check the links on our homepage. More contact and channel links are listed in the sidebar there.
